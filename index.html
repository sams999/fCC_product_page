Nutritional Powerhouse: Why Sprouts and Microgreens?

These pint-sized plants pack UP TO 40 TIMES the vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants of their full-grown counterparts! Try adding them to your next salad, smoothie, sandwich/wraps or as a healthy garnish. Sprouts and microgreens are nature's concentrated superfoods! Boost your immunity, fight free radicals, and fuel your body with every delicious bite.
